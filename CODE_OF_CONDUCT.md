# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to making participation in this project a respectful, constructive, and inclusive experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

Contributors, maintainers, and users are expected to:

- act respectfully and professionally
- focus on technical merit and evidence
- communicate clearly and constructively
- welcome feedback and review
- avoid harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks

## Unacceptable Behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- personal insults or abusive language
- harassment or discriminatory remarks
- doxxing or sharing private information
- deliberate disruption of discussions or review processes
- bad-faith submissions intended to waste reviewer time

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, issues, pull requests, commits, or other contributions that violate this code of conduct.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, commit messages, and other public interactions related to this repository.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it privately to the project maintainer before escalating publicly.
